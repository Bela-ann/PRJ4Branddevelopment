About Us

Do you wish you went to more hardrock concerts but they are too far and you do not have anyone to go with?

At 4rock create exclusive pre-concert live events 4 hardrock fans to meet and go experience the power of rock together.

Very often people from smaller towns or countries do not get the opportunity to experience big concerts of their favourite artists. We hope that by creating local communities 4rock, we can create a concert community and bring hardrock to every fan despite their location.

Many uprising artists fall unheard because they do not have a stage to scream on. Thus, you can think of our events as the opening act to the opening act where any artist or band is welcome to go up on stage and show what they are capable of.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Authencity

What are rock fans if not unconventional and outspoken? We created this brand not only to get together 4 rock but 4 you to find a place and people to scream your lungs out with on subjects that matter to you as loud as your favorite artists do.

Coming Together

Hard rock is a rebellious genre and people should not rebel alone. What is more, many people choose not to attend concerts just because they do not want to do it alone. Be part of a movement where fellow hard rock melophiles come together to experience the electrifying power of rock on a whole another level.

Passion 4 music

At 4rock we are fueled by a shared passion 4 the raw energy and unapologetic nature of hard rock music. It does not matter if you just got into the sub-genre or you have been listening to it your entire life, the pre-concert events we organise are open 4 everyone to share their passion 4 iconic riffs, loud music, powerful performances, and defiant lyrics.
