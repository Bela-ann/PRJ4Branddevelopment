    Marketing Information

    Context of campaign and promotional activities


    Our campaign will last 4 weeks and run on Instagram. Our target group is mostly active on Instagram (information obtained from the interviews we conducted as well as online research) so we will be using this platform for our campaign. TikTok has a younger demographic and will not be needed for this campaign. The goal of the campaign is to first create awareness about our Hard Rock platform and reach accounts that are interested in this music genre and events. Later on the campaign aims to introduce users to our platform and lead them to the website and turn them into active users.

    Link to our Instagram account: www.instagram.com/4rock_nl

    Message: Join and be part of a hard rock community and find like-minded people to go together with to concerts.

    Reach objective: By the 1st of April 2024 reach over 1000 accounts from our target group (hard rock fans) via Instagram. Success will be measured using analytic tools and checking KPI’s such as impressions and reach.

    Affect objective: By the 1st of April 2024 position our brand as a respectable page/brand in the hard rock community by creating a strong visual identity and social media content that gives a sense of community. Success will be measured by engagement and comments. In terms of engagement, between 1% and 3.5% is a good engagement rate for Instagram (Scrunch, 2022).

    Response objective: By the 1st of April 2024 we will reach 100 followers and an average of 40 likes per post as well as a click-through-rate to the website of 4.3 percent. Success will be measured using analytic tools and KPI’s. For B2C companies/agencies, a quarter of them witness 5 to 10 new followers per month, whereas another quarter stated they get over 100 new followers per month (Databox, 2023).

    Tactics and Strategy: We used Instagram for our promotional marketing campaign and our plan is for the first 4 weeks. We made 3 posts a week on average:

    • User-Generated Content: Make use of content created by users online (for example from hard rock concerts). This can lead to a fast growth of engagement and reach and will work for our reach objective. This type of content will be a reel as reels get 22% more interaction on Instagram than standard video posts (SocialPilot, 2024).
    • Interviews with bands/singers and event suggestions: Post interviews and clips with popular singers/bands and upcoming hard rock event suggestions that can increase trust and popularity of the brand. This will work for our affect objective leading to an increase of comments and interaction. This type of content will be a carousel post or a reel. Carousels had the highest average engagement rate of all types of Instagram posts (Israel, 2023).
    • Online Contests: Online contests for free tickets to concerts can increase the engagement and lead to more followers and click-through-rate to our website/platform. We will use Instagram Stories and regular posts.


    Content Calendar



    Learning Points

    Overall, we are satisfied with how our campaign progressed and with its results. We followed our objectives and tactics as planned before starting the campaign. The user generated content worked relatively well to gain extra followers. We expected more followers and views from this type of content. We were positively surprised by the posts featuring clips of interviews with bands/singers. These all received over 1000 views and one clip with Slash (Guns N' Roses) received 100 likes. We researched and used hashtags to expand the reach of this content. In the future we would definitely continue using clips from interviews to grow our following. We would use less UGC footage from concerts on the feed and more on Instagram Stories, as we learned that content in the feed should be of higher quality for it to reach more audience. We got positive feedback on our event suggestion designs/artworks. However, we didn't receive as much exposure and interaction from these posts as we expected. For the future we would make these posts more complete by including multiple images and videos as well instead of one single design (carousels work better for reach and engagement). The contest got some reactions, but to be even more effective, an ad can be set to reach more audience. In the future we would continue to use contests but make sure to promote them even further.


    Future Planning

    Reflection on What Worked Well:
    Throughout the marketing campaign there were a few strategies that came out to be effective.

    Interviews conducted with hard rock musicians generated quite a bit of buzz on Instagram as some of these reached more than 1000 views. These interviews helped us engage with our audience in a good way while providing useful insights into the music industry. Additionally, the giveaway we organized received some attention and positive feedback, which showed us that our audience is interested in these activities. This makes it clear that adding more giveaways to our marketing strategy in the future will be beneficial to us, although we should find sponsors or partners to make sure we reach more users through them.

    Another effective strategy was the user generated content posts. This approach helps us increase the exposure of events promoted through our platform, however we realized that these videos would have to be of higher quality to work well with the algorithm on Instagram. For our future campaigns we will gather more and more user generated content, whilst focusing on videos made with good quality camera phones opposed to video made with very old phones.

    Future Recommendations: If we were to continue the campaign, we would make sure to propose some different strategies to it based on the information we gathered so far. First, we would propose to increase our Spotify presence by creating weekly hard rock playlists. This would not only increase our brands presence on a popular music streaming platform, it would also give our followers a new and easier way to interact with our content. By including these playlists in our events, it could make the connection between our brand and our users stronger.

    Additionally, we would propose to collaborate with local music venues and promoters to boost our marketing efforts. By working together with these well-known brands in the music industry it can help us reach a new and bigger audience as well as increase our brands credibility.
    Furthermore, investing in targeted advertising on social media platforms like Facebook or Instagram will also help us reach a larger audience segment and increase the traffic coming onto our website and events.

    Process Improvements: By looking back on what we learned in this project we can see multiple areas in which we can improve our future campaigns. To begin with we recognize and see the value of conduction thorough and extensive market research for a better understanding of the behaviors and interests of our target audience. This will help us adapt our marketing strategies more effectively and increase the engagement on our website our Instagram.

    Management Information

    Lean Canvas



    Problem
    • Lack of community: As hard rock is not a mainstream genre, fans in smaller towns struggle to find establishments or events where which play exclusively rock music. Therefore, they stay at home rather than going out and meeting people who share their passion.
    • Lack of local events or awareness of them: Part of the general problem for lack of community is the lack of hard rock events or establishments in smaller towns. Often, even if there is one, it is either quite exclusive so only a few people know about it, or it is just not very popular so people miss it on their radars
    • Expensive hard rock events and geographical inconvenience: Going to hard rock events and concerts of big groups can be expensive and inconvenient to travel to, especially for people living in smaller countries or living in areas distanced from big cities where usually popular bands choose to hold their concerts.
    • Pivots (since Lean Canvas #1): The problem deviates a bit from the first lean canvas through a zoom-out pivot which indicates a general need for a local community rather than having one-time encounters for attending concerts. The initial problem remains within the second one, hence the zoom-out approach.


    Solution
    • The solution is to bring hard rock music to the fans in smaller towns and thus get them together to bond over their passion.
    • Pivots: Through the interviews, we pivoted the customer needs as we found that the users are not that averse to online communities. Although they prefer an in-person community, they also crave online social networks one that is personalized and exclusively made for them [the hard rock fans].


    Key Metrics
    • The KPIs (key performance indicators) and website click-through rate are going to measure the efficacy of the advertising efforts, while sold tickets will indicate the success of events.


    Unique Value Proposition
    • Our value proposition is formulated like this as we believe that it deals with the main problems and needs of our users in a unique and previously undeveloped solution.
    • Pivot: As the problem has a zoom-out pivot, the same applies to the value proposition as it has a more general approach to forming a hard-rock local community.


    Unfair Advantages
    • The unfair advantage stands out by catering to local fans as there is a lack of a community network for hard rock fans, therefore the market is unpenetrated. In addition, we provide events that are local and cheaper than regular hard rock big events, making them more accessible to the general public.


    Channels
    • We chose advertising on rock events and ticket services as users visit them whenever they want to attend an event. In case they see that there are no events in their area, they can choose to attend a local event organized by us. In addition, we implement Instagram as a distribution channel as it is the platform most used by our customer segment.
    • Pivot: The website has been removed as an advertising channel since it is the product being advertised rather than a means for advertising. We added advertising on ticket sales services as the zoom-out pivot of the concept provides an opportunity to advertising efforts as the solution is to create events.


    Customer segment
    • Late millennials (born in the 1990s) and early GenZs (born in the early 2000s) are a generation that has grown up during years fueled by nostalgia for the hard rock music of the 80s. As hard rock lovers are usually not fond of regular bars which tend to play pop music which caters to most visitors, especially in smaller towns, our value proposition is personalized to perfectly answer the needs of this age group. We have also included uprising rock bands as another customer segment as they will be the centre of the events we organize and in return, we give them an opportunity to play and gain fans. Our final segment is bar owners in smaller towns looking to expand their business by hosting our events. As hard rock and metal fans are very loyal (Stafford, 2015), we could provide those bars with clients that keep coming back in return for their venue.
    • Pivot: We made a customer segment pivot in expanding to rock bands and bar owners. In addition, we made user segment adjustments as we found that rock is more popular among older millennials therefore for them is easier to find buddies, moreover, they are not in big need of companionship.


    Revenue Streams
    • Freemium revenue model with a possibility for a monthly subscription of 5 euros a month - After conducting solution interviews, we found that users are willing to pay a subscription to the website and brand if it comes with added value during the events. For instance, subscribing should provide free entrance to events or a discount for drinks.
    • Affiliate marketing - Our brand can collaborate with events, concerts, bars, and bands to generate website traffic and ticket sales.
    • Advertising - The brand is going to undertake marketing and advertising efforts on the designated distribution channels.
    • Sales revenue model - Selling tickets for the organised events, average price is 5 euros per ticket which includes a band performance (solution interviews found that users would not be willing to pay for a ticket if there is no performance involved). As the bands are emerging and looking for awareness, we expect them to perform for free with complimentary drinks, therefore keeping the events affordable. Some events can be of bigger bands which would increase the price of tickets and allow for a higher margin.


    Lean Canvas


    Focus on in-person rather than online community:

    When greeting the solution to our problem, we assumed that hard rock fans would prefer to get together in person and let them bond over their passion for concerts and music, rather than focusing on creating a community online.

    Validation:

    Our assumption proved to be true after conducting the solution interviews as the interviewees confirmed that bringing concerts to the fans is a strong concept that addresses and fosters the community better as it gives it a situation to develop. In addition, it does not only create a community between the fans but also with the bands and local businesses that are part of creating the events.



    Emphasize on website rather than social media:

    When coming up with a solution for an online platform, we decided to focus on a website as it creates more personalized, trustworthy, and exclusive content rather than focusing our efforts on social media. We assumed that hard rock fans are not really active on social media and they would prefer to have a place dedicated to hard rock news, events, and community, rather than having to scroll through social media to get updates.

    Validation:

    After conducting our problem and solution interviews, the answers of our interviewees proved our assumptions to be right as they said that they would prefer a separate app (that represents the website) rather than a typical social media structure as it addresses the community more effectively. This execution gives a more personal and reliable touch to the product and brand.
